Welcome to the Berkeley Student Cooperative!

The BSC is a nonprofit housing cooperative in Berkeley, California that provides low-cost housing to undergraduate and graduate students at colleges and universities across the Bay Area. The cooperative is comprised of 17 houses and 3 apartment buildings which house over 1200 students. Each co-op offers unique history and traditions which are reflected in its proud members.
